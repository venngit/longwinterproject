Auto-Reply

Collection of Short Poems & Monochromes

By Venn Vaillant

Status: Published

This poetry collection explores the quiet yet powerful moments when the heart "auto-replies" to life - moved by a story, a distant memory, the ache of loss, or the struggles of love.

Accompanied by soothing monochrome visuals captured by the author, Auto-Reply invites readers to experience their own moments of love, loss, and introspection. Perfect for those who find peace in solitude and reflection on past struggles.

This book offers an intimate space to connect life's deeply personal moments, however brief.

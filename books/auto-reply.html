Auto-Reply

Collection of Short Poems & Monochromes

By Venn Vaillant

Status: Published

Auto-Reply is an intimate collection that moves between short-form poetry and monochrome visual pieces. The writing is concise but emotionally direct, built from quiet observations and sharp pauses that feel like messages left in the middle of a long winter.

Each piece is designed to be read slowly. Instead of long narrative arcs, the book focuses on fragments of feeling, memory, and stillness. The monochrome elements reinforce that tone, giving the pages a restrained rhythm where language and negative space can breathe together.

The collection invites readers who are drawn to minimal expression, compact imagery, and reflective reading sessions. It is less about explanation and more about atmosphere: small texts that linger after the page is turned.
